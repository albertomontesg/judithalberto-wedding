Where?

We’re getting married at Mas Torroella, a 14th Century Catalan manor house which is provided by several gardens, an outdoor swimming pool, a tennis court, a spa and a sauna.

Everything we need to spend together an unforgettable weekend!

Mas Torroella is located at Camí de Fitor, between Palafrugell i Forallac.

Since the way to the venue may be complicated, we have organised a few buses to facilitate the transportation of the guests. You can find more information at the section Transport.

Events

The ceremony, the reception and the big party will take place at Mas Torroella. In addition, on the next day we will have a delicious lunch together: paella! Here you can find information about the different events and everything you need to know.

  • Ceremony and reception

    • Mas Torroella, Camí de Fitor, s/n, Palafrugell
    • 17:00h - 24:00h, 12th September 2020

    The ceremony will begin at 17:00h. While waiting for the bride and groom to arrive, water and juice will be offered to the guests to fight the heat.

    The cocktail will take place after the ceremony and will be followed by the reception. We’ve prepared multiple suprises for you. Find them out during the cocktail!

  • Party

    • Mas Torroella, Camí de Fitor, s/n, Palafrugell
    • 0:00h - 6:00h, 13th September 2020

    Around midnight the party will begin, and it will last until the body holds!

    To make sure that you can enjoy the party without worrying too much about the drinks, we have organised several buses at different times of the night. They will bring you to the accommodation places.

  • Paella

    • Mas Torroella, Camí de Fitor, s/n, Palafrugell
    • 12:00h - 18:00h, 13th September 2020

    On the next day, a delicious paella will be waiting for us!

    We have also prepared some activities to enjoy the swimming pool, the tennis court, the table-tennis court and the various facilities of the house.

Counting Down to Our Big Day

Accommodation

In order to make everything as simple as possible, we have made sure that all of our guests can benefit from free accommodation at the bungalows of Camping La Siesta. However, if you would rather pay for your accommodation somewhere else, we suggest Hotel Arrels d’Empordà, which will offer you some discounts.

Free Accommodation

The bungalows at Camping La Siesta, in Calella de Palafrugell, are a great choice due to their good location and nice facilities.

The camping, at 5min from the beach, has a swimming pool, a fitness area, a supermarket, bars, and everything you may need.

Remember:
Bungalows are similar to a hotel room. They all have their own kitchen and bathroom and, therefore, the bed sheets as well as the kitchen utensils are provided. You don’t need to bring anything!

How to arrive

Alternative Accommodation

The Aparthotel Arrels d’Empordà****, in Palafrugell, offers bedrooms and apartments for 1 to 5 people. The prices are really affordable, but to be able to profit from the discounts, you should mention our wedding and book your room by 26/06/2020.

If you need specific information about the prices, please, contact us.

Remember:
This accommodation is an alternative and, therefore, it will not be for free!

How to arrive

Transport

To facilitate the transport of all guests, a few buses will take you back and forth between Mas Torroella and the accomodation places.

IMPORTANT: BUS TIMETABLES MAY VARY. PLEASE, CHECK THIS INFORMATION AGAIN AT A LATER DATE!

Buses to the Venue

Several buses will pick you up at the main entrance of Camping La Siesta, in Calella de Palafrugell. Depending on the number of guests staying at the alternative accommodation, the buses might also make a stop at the hotel.

The buses will leave at 16:30h!

Buses back to the accommodation

These buses will drive you from Mas Torroella to Camping La Siesta or to the entrance of the alternative accomodation. The buses will leave at different times.

Estimated times: 1:30h, 2:30h, 3:00h, 4:00h, 5:00h, 5:30h.

Important

Like in any event, there are a few aspects to keep in mind. Please, read them carefully.

  • Dressing

    • Please, do not dress in red! But you can dress in white!

    • We recommend that the women wear comfortable heels or spare shoes, since much of the gardens are lawns with some gravel paths between different areas.

    • If you come to the “paella” of the following day, we recommend you to bring the swimming suit and wear comfortable clothes.

  • Food

    • In case of food allergies, please inform us accordingly.

    • If you would rather have a vegetarian/vegan menu, please mention it in the form when confirming your attendance.

  • Deadlines

    • Please, confirm your attendance by 1st June 2020.

    • In case you prefer the alternative accommodation, the booking should be made by 26th June 2020 in order to benefit from the discounts.
