Where?

We're getting married at Mas Torroella, a 14th Century Catalan manor house which is provided by several gardens, an outdoor swimming pool, a tennis court, a spa and a sauna.

Everything we need to spend together an unforgettable weekend!

Mas Torroella is located at Camí de Fitor, between Palafrugell and Forallac.

Since the way to the venue may be complicated, we have organised a few buses to facilitate the transportation of the guests. You can find more information at the section Transport .

The ceremony, the reception and the big party will take place at Mas Torroella. In addition, on the previous day we will organize a party by the beach! Here you can find information about the different events and everything you need to know.

Accommodation

In order to make everything as simple as possible, we recommend you different types of accomodation. All of them are close to the wedding place, and our wedding buses will also bring you / pick you up there (or very close).

You will have to book your desired accommodation yourselves. However, we are happy to assist you in everything you may need.

Hotel ****

The Aparthotel Arrels d'Empordà**** (in Palafrugell) offers bedrooms and apartments for 1 to 5 people. You can benefit from a 5% discount with the code: JUDITH&ALBERTO23. To be able to profit from the discounts, you should book your room before 01/05/2023.

If you need specific information about prices, please, contact us.

Remember: The minimum stay required is 2 nights.

How to arrive

Hostel

Solidança Hostel (in Palafrugell) has multiple accomodation options, from individual rooms to up to 7-bed rooms. You can choose staying in rooms with private shower or shared bathroom.

The hostel is a referent of responsible tourism based on sustainability criteria.

Remember: If you stay at the hostel, the bed sheets as well as the kitchen utensils are provided. You don't need to bring anything!

How to arrive

Camping and Bungalows

The bungalows at Camping Calella (in Calella de Palafrugell) are a great choice due to their good location and nice facilities.

The camping, at 5min from the beach, has a swimming pool, a fitness area, a supermarket, bars, and everything you may need.

Remember: If you stay at the bungalows, the bed sheets as well as the kitchen utensils are provided. You don't need to bring anything!

Transport

To facilitate the transport of all guests, a few buses will take you back and forth between Mas Torroella and the accomodation places.

Buses to the Venue

Several buses will pick you up at these two stops:

  • In Calella de Palafrugell, the main entrance of Camping Calella.
  • In Palafrugell, the LIDL supermarket (next to Plaça d'Europa).

The buses will leave at 16:30h from Calella and at 16:35h from Palafrugell!

Buses back to the accommodation

The buses will drive you from Mas Torroella to these two stops:

  • In Calella de Palafrugell, the main entrance of Camping Calella.
  • In Palafrugell, the LIDL supermarket (next to Plaça d'Europa).

The buses will leave from Mas Torroela at: 1:00h, 2:30h, 3:30h, 4:00h, 5:30h.

Important

Like in any event, there are a few aspects to keep in mind. Please, read them carefully.

  • Dressing

    • Please, do not dress in red! But you can dress in white, if you want!

    • We recommend that the women wear comfortable heels or spare shoes, since some of the gardens are lawns with gravel paths between different areas.

    • If you come to the pre-wedding party, we recommend you to bring your swimsuit!

  • Food

    • In case of food allergies, please inform us accordingly.

    • If you would rather have a vegetarian/vegan menu, please mention it in the form when confirming your attendance.

  • Deadlines

    • Please, confirm your attendance by 1st April 2023.
    • If you would like to benefit from discounts in the accommodation, remmember to book your room before 1st May 2023.
